Description of Beans

Description of Beans

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Palm Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash the beans
  2. Put water on fire and add the washed beans
  3. Slice Onions in to the beans and leave to cook till beans is soft
  4. While cooking the beans, blend pepper and onions
  5. Pour the blended pepper and onion into the cooked the beans, add seasoning, add salt to taste and leave for some minutes
  6. Stir the Beans and food is ready
